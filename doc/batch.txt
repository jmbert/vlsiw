A Batch is 256 bits, made up of 5 Instructions and metadata:
	Template: 36 bits
		3-bit Format * 5: 15 bits
		1 Stop bit
		20 Reserved bits
	5 Instructions (44 bits each) for the formats

Undefined Formats:
	Formats with more than one branch
	Formats with any reserved formats
